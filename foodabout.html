ABOUT US ?

FOODARA is a restaurant that has been serving authentic Andhra cuisine since 2006. What started as a small restaurant in Koramangala, Bangalore has now grown into a well-known name for serving quality food with love.
FOODARA menu also has authentic Hyderabadi Biryani. Their unique chicken biryani is served with succulent chicken pieces (boneless) that will tantalize your taste buds.

FOODARA is popular among Bangaloreans for its great taste. The restaurant now has five outlets in the city Koramangala, Singasandra, Marathahalli, Jayanagar, and Residency Road. You may also order food online on Swiggy or Zomato from FOODARA and await a food delivery at your doorstep.
